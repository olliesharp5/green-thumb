Welcome to Green Thumb

Your one-stop shop for all your gardening needs.

Our website boasts a vast selection of garden flora, ideal for your flower beds, hanging pots, and patio planters. We offer a variety of Roses, Bamboos and Grasses, along with hedging and screening plants, and fruit trees like apples, pears, cherries, plums, and even figs. Additionally, we cultivate a wide array of hardy herbaceous perennials, ferns, shrubs, and climbers, with Clematis, Hydrangea, and Lavender Plants being among the top picks. You'll also find Palms, Buxus Box Topiary, Bay Trees, Olives, and Specimen plants. Altogether, we cultivate countless hardy plants at our Essex nursery, which spans over 14 acres. That's an immense amount of plants! From Garden Plants, Shrubs, Trees, Patio Plants and more, we're all about them. The pillars of our business are quality, service, and value for money. We operate our own nurseries and collaborate closely with other plant enthusiasts across the gardening community to bring you the finest new and exclusive plants, beloved classics, and the latest trends. We're proud to have been chosen to supply plants for high-profile RHS Flower Shows, BBC Gardeners World live, the Corporation of London, celebrity chefs, stately homes, and many others.
